## Motivate
**Why am I doing this? What is my goal?** \
I struggle to write at whim because I cannot structurally think about it because I understand grammar intuitively but not as rule that I can apply alongside a thought. Also, there is some trauma from poor performance in my English 1 from middle and junior high school. Thus, it could be a confounding reason for my poor cohesion and coherence suggesting a confidence issue. My primary goal is to be a better student of English language. By this I mean, know exactly how to decompress a sentence into fundamentals, precisely understand the intention of any given text with ease, and also, think and write my thoughts succinctly.

**How will I be tested?** \
In this self learning class I will be tested mainly on textbook exercises and writing quality which is judged by a LLM or online forums.

**What is my study plan?** \
I hope to take a very practical and application-focused approach. I want to make sure I can competently describe English grammar at a high level so I should continuously test myself on that
